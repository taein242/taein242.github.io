Awards & Honors

2025.08

Emerging Psychologist Award

Annual Conference of the Korean Psychological Association
Title : Are Fair People Less Happy? Effects of Empathy and Subjective Status on the Relationship Between Social Preferences and Happiness.

Lee, T. (Presenter), Kwon, Y., Bang, J., & Sul, S.

How happiness is related with individuals' consideration for the welfare of themselves and others? This poster presentation shows that individuals with higher empathic concern tend to consider less advantaged others more in resource distribution situations. This tendency was more pronounced among those with higher subjective socioeconomic status and was associated with lower life satisfaction.
